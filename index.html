QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT - Q3 2024

Wilson & Associates is pleased to report strong financial performance for the third quarter of 2024. Our total revenue reached $127.5 million, representing a significant increase from the previous quarter.

Operating expenses for the quarter totaled $89.2 million, which includes a one-time infrastructure investment of $15.7 million. Our gross profit margin improved to 38.3%, exceeding our projected target of 35%.

The Technology division contributed $45.8 million in revenue, while our Consulting services generated $52.1 million. The remaining $29.6 million came from our Strategic Advisory services.

Employee headcount increased by 247 professionals this quarter, bringing our total workforce to 12,459 employees globally. Research and development investments totaled $18.3 million, representing 14.4% of our total revenue.

Looking ahead to Q4, we project revenue growth of approximately 12.8%, with total anticipated revenue of $143.8 million. Our cash reserves stand at $892.1 million, providing strong financial stability for future expansion initiatives.
